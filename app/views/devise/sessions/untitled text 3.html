Pygmy Marmoset

Pygmy marmosets can rotate their head 180 degrees!

  • Scientific Name: Cebuella pygmaea
  • Average Weight: 100 grams (3.5 oz)
  • Geographic Range: Amazon Basin
  • Diet: Tree gum, fruit, and insects

One of the world's smallest primates, the pygmy marmoset lives in evergreen forests in the Amazon Basin, often near rivers. These social animals live in small groups of up to nine members. These family groups have an intricate communication system. Despite their small size, their low-frequency calls reveraberate througout the forest.
